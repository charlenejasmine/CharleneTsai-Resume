Hello. My name is Charlene Tsai. I am currently a fourth-year Psychology student at the University of California, Riverside. I am particularly interested in developmental psychology, specifically brain development in infants.

I am currently a research assistant at one of UC Riverside's child development labs. I have been working in this lab for the past three years. Through this experience, I had the chance to explore my interest in this field of study. Upon graduating, I will be attending graduate school to earn my masters in Child Developmental Psychology. Then, I plan to come back to UC Riverside in order to obtain my PH.D. in Psychology.

Aside from working as a research assistant, I spent my summers working as a Direct Support Professional in a care home facility. I was tasked with taking care of individuals with mental and physical disabilities. My tasks included cooking, cleaning, and assisting individuals during their daily activities. My work experience also includes working as a medical receptionist for an acupuncture clinic.
